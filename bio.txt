Stephen O'Connell is Associate Professor of Economics at Emory University. His current research focuses on the design and evaluation of social protection programs in low-income countries. His work has been published in journals in a wide range of disciplines including economics, political science, and global health. He is an Affiliate of the IZA Institute of Labor Economics and Households in Conflict Network, and regularly advises for and partners with the United Nations, the World Bank, and other agencies.
